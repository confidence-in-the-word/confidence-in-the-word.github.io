  In This Section...

Although by now you may be thoroughly convinced that the Bible is genuinely the Word of God yet you may still be hesitant about using it by yourself. In this section we invite you to test drive the Bible for yourself, by exploring some representative portions of Scripture using a variety of methods. We have called these five exercises Bible Focus.

However, before we come to these, we start by encouraging you with a remarkable testimony of how a Bristol lady was healed of a debilitating illness by personally interacting with the Scriptures, and follow this by offer two wide-ranging exercises to motivate you to do the same.

There is, of course, no obligation to tackle everything we offer! Simply choose whatever is most appropriate for your own particular interests and concerns.

As a  result of visiting the pages this section we hope you will come to see that the Bible is a book that can be readily understood and applied to daily life by every Christian.
