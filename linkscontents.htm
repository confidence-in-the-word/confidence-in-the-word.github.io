  In This Section...

We sincerely hope that Confidence in the Word has helped you develop a greater measure of assurance when personally interacting with the Scriptures. In this section we invite you to explore some of the valuable work being done by some others to the same end. The author recognises that his own efforts are merely as a drop in the ocean. Even so, he takes comfort from the fact that without many such drops there would be no ocean!

Would you like to read a Bible passage in different versions or languages, or to study the Scriptures more deeply? Are you interested in Bible work around the world and how you can become involved? Would you like to to listen to Christian radio or catch-up on the latest Church news? Or perhaps you are interested in the source of some of our illustrations? These are just some of the links to be found here.

The links we offer are arranged alphabetically within each of the seven pages of this section.

  A Word of Caution

While Confidence in the Word has every confidence in the integrity of the providers of the websites to which links are given, we regret we can neither accept responsibility for the information displayed on them, nor for the contents of any sites to which they may offer links.

When visiting other websites we therefore urge you always to apply 1 Thessalonians 5:21-22 (NIV) and 1 John 4:1-3.
