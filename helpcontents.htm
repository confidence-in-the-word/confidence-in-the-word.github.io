 In This Section...

Have you decided to start, or to start again on reading the Bible regularly, but simply don't know where to begin? Then may we suggest a visit to the first page in this section?

Or are you thinking of investing in some Bible-related resources - perhaps a dictionary or an atlas? Or are you confused by the variety of concordances on offer? Our second page in this section is full of helpful advice.

Or perhaps you feel in need of some challenging thoughts about the Scriptures to kick-start your reading? We guarantee the third page here will get you thinking.

The final page offers a challenge from the past which is still relevant today, together with a promise from the Lord to everyone who reads and acts upon his Word and two prayers you make like to use either before or after reading the Scriptures.
