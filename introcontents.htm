  In This Section...

Confidence in the Word has been written out of the conviction that many Christians no longer read the Bible because they have suffered a fundamental loss of confidence in the Scriptures.

After first directing you to a Site Map, which offers a bird's eye view of the entire website, we begin by reviewing the research by two Christian organisations that led to Confidence in the Word being written, and follow this with an explanation of the method we have endeavoured to follow as far as is possible, of allowing the Bible to speak for itself. This first section ends with a short biography of the author, his hopes and his dreams.
