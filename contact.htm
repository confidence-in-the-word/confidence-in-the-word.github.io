Has Confidence in the Word encouraged you to begin, or to make a fresh start, to read the Bible? Or has it helped you in some other way? It would be such an encouragement to us to know that it has.

Perhaps you still have some reservations or questions regarding the Scriptures? Or would you like some further explanation about something we have written? If so, please contact us and we promise to do our best to deal with your query or difficulty.

Or do you feel that something we have said on this website is not entirely accurate? Is one of the links not working properly? Again, please let us know and we'll do our best to put things right.

All correspondence will be treated in the strictest confidence, and we shall contact you only if you ask us to do so. We promise that we shall never ask you for a financial contribution. All we ask is that you remember Confidence in the Word in your prayers; that the Lord of the Scriptures will be pleased to use the website to assist many in developing their love for the Bible and the Saviour of which it speaks.

 A Closing Thought

May the Lord keep you in your logging-out and your logging-in! Leave with the confident assurance that The Lord keeps watch over you as you come and go, both now and forever (Psalm 121:5-8). And may your own confidence in the Word continue to grow every time you read the Bible, the most precious book the world has to offer.
