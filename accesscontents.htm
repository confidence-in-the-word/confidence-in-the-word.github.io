 In This Section...

If you have never read the Bible before, or if you have not used one for some time, then this section is the place to start. As well as offering some guidance to help you find your way around the Bible, we offer lists of all the books in the Old and New Testaments plus a page of information about the Apocrypha.

We then try to address the all-important issue of whose word the Bible really is, and conclude the section with some guidelines to help you start reading the Bible in the right way.
