 In This Section...

The Bible makes many remarkable claims for itself. Can they be justified or verified? Where did the Bible come from and how did it come to be in its present form? What did the Bible writers themselves think about the Scriptures? Does the Bible have a connecting theme throughout? How easy or difficult is the Bible to use? How did Jesus use the Scriptures of his time?

In this section we have tried to address these questions by highlighting some of the things the Bible says about itself, and how our Lord Jesus Christ spoke about and used the Scriptures. By answering such questions ourselves, we have come to the conclusion that the Bible is the unique, authoritative and completely reliable Word of God. We hope that as a result of visiting the pages in this section you will come to share this belief as well.
