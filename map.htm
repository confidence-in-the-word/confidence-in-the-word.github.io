 Navigating the Site

While you are naturally at liberty to visit the pages of Confidence in the Word in any order you choose, we believe you will gain the greatest benefit from your visit by working through the pages in the order shown above. To assist you in this, the Next Page link at the foot of each page directs you to the next page in sequence, whether the next page in the section, or, in the case of the last page of a section, the first  page (Contents) for the next section. The Previous Page link directs you to the page before, or, in the case of the first  page (Contents) of a section, the last page of the previous section. Each page also has links returning you directly to the Home Page and the Site Map.

On some pages we refer you back to an item on a earlier page, or forward to a section of a later page. These links are purely optional and are intended only as means of explaining or amplifying a point. At the end of the section to which you are referred, you will find a further link directing you back to the place on the page from which you have come. We refer to this as returning to the Main Text.

We have also provided a range of external links. With only a very few exceptions, these are to be found in the Links pages. In order to avoid these pages becoming unduly cumbersome we have not provided return links to other pages within the site. You will therefore need to click on the Back button on your browser in order to return to the Main Text. However, we have targeted all external links, so you can return directly to this site simply by clicking the Close button in the top right hand corner of your browser irrespective where you are in the site you are visiting (i.e. you do no have to go back through all the pages you have visited).

All Bible references in Confidence in the Word are given in the form of live links to Bible Gateway, where you can view the Scriptures in a wide variety of English versions and foreign languages. A selection of books from the Apocrypha may be viewed in the NETBible, which also includes the entire Old and New Testaments. Our use of these facilities is gratefully acknowledged (see Acknowledgements & Thanks).
